Beer Steak

Grilled Steak with a simple beer marinade!

As the All-Father commands, all our warriors will feast with beer as an ingredient in every dish! Honor him, follow this recipe:

  • 4 Pounds of the Valhallan Wagyu
  • 3 tablespoons of salt
  • 4 tablespoons of lemmon pepper
  • 2 Bottles of the finest mead in Valhalla, either Thor's Special Homebrew or Loki's Pale Ale
Step 1

Season your Valhallan Wagyu with the salt and lemon pepper. Don't hold back on the seasoning, or you risk invoking Thor's Wrath!

Step 2

Place your Valhallan Wagyu in a large, shallow container with a lid. Gently pour your Valhallan Ale of choice, with care not too wash off the seasoning thus invoking Loki's laughter, don't give him the oportunity to question your chef skills.

Step 3

Pre-heat the grill, or ask Thor to ready Mjollnir.

Step 4

Place steaks on the grill, for 5 mins each side, or to the desired doneness. If Thor did fire up Mjollnir, 1 smack per side of the steak should have you at medium rare. 2 blows for medium, and with 3 it's ruined. Discard of the marinade.
